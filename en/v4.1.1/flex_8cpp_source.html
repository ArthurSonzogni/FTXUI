99/// @brief Make a child element to expand proportionnally to the space left in a
100/// container.
101/// @ingroup dom
102///
103/// #### Examples:
104///
105/// ~~~cpp

108/// text("middle") | border | flex,

113/// #### Output:

118/// └────┘└─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘└─────┘
